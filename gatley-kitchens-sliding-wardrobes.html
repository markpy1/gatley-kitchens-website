Why Choose Sliding Wardrobes?

Space-Saving Design Meets Modern Style

Sliding wardrobes offer the perfect solution for bedrooms where space is at a premium. Unlike traditional hinged doors that require clearance to open, sliding wardrobes glide smoothly along tracks, making them ideal for smaller rooms or awkward layouts.

At Gatley Kitchens & Bedrooms, we design and fit sliding wardrobes tailored to your exact requirements. Whether you're looking to fill an alcove, create a floor-to-ceiling storage wall, or simply modernise your bedroom, our bespoke solutions are crafted with precision and care.

Maximise Your Space

Sliding doors require no clearance space, making them perfect for compact bedrooms or tight corners where traditional doors won't work.

Bespoke to Your Room

Every wardrobe is measured, designed and built to fit your exact space — from alcoves to full-width bedroom walls.

Quality Materials

Choose from a wide range of door finishes including mirror, glass, wood effect, and high-gloss panels to complement your bedroom style.
