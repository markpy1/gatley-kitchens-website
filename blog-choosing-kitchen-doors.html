Choosing the Right Style of Kitchen Door

Shaker, handleless, slab — what actually suits your home, and how to avoid choosing something you'll regret.

When designing a kitchen, choosing the right door style is crucial in achieving the desired look and functionality of the space. There are various door styles to choose from, each with its unique characteristics and benefits.

Shaker Doors

Shaker Doors remain a popular choice for many homeowners due to their clean and simple lines. They feature a square frame surrounding a flat centre panel, making them an excellent option for homeowners who want a traditional or transitional look for their kitchen.

Slab Doors

Slab Doors feature a smooth, flat surface with no surrounding frame or panel. They are a popular choice for modern kitchens due to their minimalist appearance. Slab doors can be made from a variety of materials, including wood, laminate, or acrylic.

Handleless Doors

Handleless Doors are a modern and minimalist option that have become increasingly popular in recent years. They eliminate the need for handles or knobs, creating a streamlined and sleek appearance. They can be paired with any of the above door styles, including Shaker or slab, to achieve a contemporary look for the kitchen.

Choosing what's right for you

When choosing a door style for your kitchen, consider the overall style of your home and select a style that complements it. For example, a Shaker door style may work well in a traditional or transitional home, while handleless doors may be more suited to a modern or contemporary home.

It is also important to consider the material of the doors. Solid wood doors may be more expensive, but they offer a high-end look and feel. Whilst vinyl wrapped doors won't last as long as solid wood, they are a great option at a much more affordable price point.

Finally, consider the functionality of the doors. Do you need handleless doors for a sleek and modern look? Or do you prefer a more traditional look with Shaker doors? Consider your specific needs and preferences when selecting a door style for your kitchen.

In conclusion, choosing the right door style for your kitchen is essential in creating a beautiful and functional space. Shaker, slab, and handleless doors are popular options, each offering unique benefits that can be tailored to meet your specific needs and preferences.
