Island Life: The Latest Kitchen Island Trends

Kitchen islands have become one of the most requested features we design — here's what's trending right now.

Kitchen islands have become a popular feature in modern kitchens, as they provide additional counter space, storage, and seating options. Here are some of the latest trends with kitchen islands.

Multifunctional Islands

Kitchen islands are no longer just for food preparation. Homeowners are now opting for multifunctional islands that serve as a workspace, storage area, and even a dining area. Islands with built-in sinks, stovetops, and wine refrigerators are becoming increasingly popular, as they allow for easy meal preparation and entertaining.

Large Islands

Homeowners are also opting for larger kitchen islands to accommodate their growing families and entertaining needs. Large islands provide ample space for food preparation, dining, and even homework or office work. They also add a grand focal point to the kitchen, making a statement with their size and style.

Curved or Angled Islands

Straight lines are no longer the only option when it comes to kitchen islands. Curved or angled islands are becoming more popular, as they add visual interest and break up the monotony of straight lines in the kitchen. Curved or angled islands also provide a softer look and can create a more fluid traffic flow in the kitchen.

Hidden Appliances

Another trend with kitchen islands is the incorporation of hidden appliances. Homeowners are now hiding appliances such as dishwashers, waste disposal units and microwaves within the island. This not only provides a seamless look to the kitchen but also frees up valuable counter space and reduces visual clutter.

Statement Lighting

Finally, statement lighting is another trend when it comes to kitchen islands. Pendant lights are a popular choice, as they can provide both ambient and task lighting. Oversized chandeliers, industrial-style lighting, and even string lights are also being used to create a unique and stylish look in the kitchen.

In conclusion, kitchen islands are a great way to add functionality and style to any kitchen. Multifunctional islands, large islands, curved or angled islands, hidden appliances, and statement lighting are some of the latest trends when it comes to kitchen islands. Consider incorporating one or more of these trends into your kitchen island design to create a beautiful and functional space.
