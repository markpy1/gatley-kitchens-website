Customer Care Information for Vinyl Wrapped Doors

Our doors come with an average of 6 years warranty so you can rest assured that they are of the highest quality.

Not a lot of people are aware of how to care and maintain them, so we put together this guide with some do's and don'ts.

This is the same for any vinyl door from any supplier
(yes even those expensive ones that cost 4x the price!)
The difference is... we tell you about it!
🔥

Oven

Modern ovens are fitted with heat deflectors, however we can also fit heat deflection strips if required.

🍽️

Dishwasher

For new installations, always fit a heat & moisture shield above dishwashers to prevent doors coming into contact with concentrated steam.

☕

Kettle

Never allow concentrated steam (e.g. from a kettle) to be released directly underneath a vinyl wrapped door.

💨

Extractor / Integrated Hood

Doors must not be fitted any less than 750mm above a hob and extraction doors must always be opened whilst the hob is in use.

🍞

Toaster

Never use appliances that generate heat, such as a toaster, directly underneath a vinyl wrapped door.

💧

Water

Never allow a vinyl wrapped door to become saturated with water.

Cleaning Instructions

We recommend the following door cleaning instructions for all of our doors:

✓ DO

  • Clean the doors using only a solution of 95% lukewarm water with no more than 5% soap
  • Use a clean damp (not wet) cloth
  • Wipe any cooking marks immediately
  • Dry with a soft, clean cloth
  • Wipe any spillages immediately, especially at door joints

✗ DON'T

  • Spray anything directly onto your doors (this includes the cleaning solution recommended)
  • Allow liquids to seep into joints of doors
  • Use wax furniture polish
  • Use bleach or chlorine-based cleaning products
  • Use abrasive cleaners or scouring pads
  • Use solvents or multi-purpose cleaners
  • Use alcohol-based cleaners
  • Use ammonia or any other similar product

With the correct care, your doors will give you many years of trouble-free service.
