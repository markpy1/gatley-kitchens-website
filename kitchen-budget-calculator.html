What will your new

Answer seven questions about your kitchen and we'll give you a realistic ballpark figure — based on the actual materials and suppliers we use every day.

Kitchen Estimate Step 1 of 7
Step 1 — Kitchen Size

How big is your
kitchen space?

Think about how many walls you have units running along, not just the floor area. A narrow room can still have a lot of storage if units run on two long walls.

Step 2 — Layout

What shape is
the layout?

Layout affects how many units are needed and whether specialist corner solutions are required. Corner units cost more and take longer to fit.

Step 3 — Door Style

What kind of
door style?

All our doors are made-to-measure to the exact size of each unit — no standard sizes, no unnecessary gaps. Your door choice affects both the look and the cost.

Step 4 — Colour & Finish

What colour
are you thinking?

Our doors cover everything from classic whites and creams through to bold colours, wood effects, and fully bespoke painted finishes. We bring samples on the home visit.

Step 5 — Worktops

What worktop
material?

Worktop choice has a significant impact on the overall budget. Our stone worktops are templated and fitted by our specialist supplier — we coordinate everything.

Step 6 — Installation Complexity

How straightforward
is the installation?

Some jobs are simple swap-outs. Others involve moving pipes, rerouting electrics, building in appliances, or working around difficult ceiling heights or uneven walls.

Step 7 — Existing Kitchen

Is there an existing
kitchen to remove?

Stripping out an old kitchen adds time to the job. We remove and dispose of the old units as part of the installation — you don't need to arrange a skip separately.

What's included in our fitting price: delivery of all units, installation of carcasses, doors and drawers, fitting of plinths and cornices, coordination with your plumber and electrician, and a snagging visit after completion. We use a trusted fitter we've worked with for years — not a subcontractor we've never met.

Important — please read before getting in touch

  • These figures are a ballpark only. Every kitchen is different and an accurate price can only be given after Mark visits your home, takes measurements, and understands exactly what you have in mind. There's no charge for this visit.
  • Appliances are not included. Ovens, hobs, extractors, dishwashers, and fridges are supplied and fitted by your own appliance retailer or electrician. We design around whatever you're buying.
  • Plumbing and electrics are not included. We coordinate with your plumber and electrician, but their work is a separate cost. Most of our customers use their own trusted trades for this.
  • Tiling, flooring, plastering, and decorating are not included. These are typically done before or after the kitchen installation by your own trades.
  • If your kitchen involves significant structural changes — e.g. removing a wall, moving a soil stack, or adding drainage in an extension — these are separate costs handled by a builder or plumber before we start.
  • All prices shown include VAT at the current standard rate of 20%. Fitting is charged without VAT where applicable to help keep your costs down.
  • These figures reflect kitchens installed across South Manchester — Cheadle, Gatley, Didsbury, Hale, Bramhall, and surrounding areas — using the specific suppliers and materials we work with regularly.
